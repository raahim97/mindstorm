About Us


  • We provide platform for students to practice questions regarding four of the major programming subjects.
  • Students first needs to signup and create his/her account in order to attempt quiz of any subject.
  • Students can view his/her marks for the regarding quiz right after attempting the quiz.
  • Students will be awarded badges as per their performance in the quizzes to encourage them.
  • Helping material regardin the programming subjects will also be privided to the students to enhance their learning.
  • Track record of every quiz attempted by the student is maintained in order observe his/her performance.
  • Teacher first needs to sign up in order to get access to the functionalities such as adding cource and questions.
  • Admin approves the request of the teacher in order to maintain the security and authenticity of the website
  • After the approval of the request, teacher can add/remove course and questions from any specific cource.
  • Teacher can add course/exam to system and questions to that course.
  • Teacher can view marks of every student to access his/her academic performance.
  • Feedback system is also provided to the students if they have any question regarding the Web Application.
